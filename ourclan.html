Show Titles and Awards

Past and Present Show titles awarded to our gerbils. This includes shows we have attended in person and any virtual shows we entered for fun. Although virtual show titles do not count toward our gerbils' championships, we show them off here for fun as well!


VIRTUAL SHOW 2013


Pied Class, Third Place

Awarded to RL CC Dallas August 31st 2013

Judges comments

Gorgeous and well prepared for the show. Fantastic pied markings with ideal pied markings including of white on the tail tip and very full white collar. Nice shape to the ears and eyes. May be the lighting but coloring looks uneven - might he be a dark patched pied? Eyes appear black rather than ruby. Wonderful body type, straight tail and nice tuft. Clean and beautiful, nicely posed."


Pet Class, Honourable Mention

Awarded to RL CC Pebble August 31st 2013

Judges comments

"The far-away look in this gerbil's eyes make me wonder if he or she is dreaming about Mongolia...the moiré pattern reflection from the glass adds to the dreamy effect. A beautiful animal with a lovely mottling pattern, too."
