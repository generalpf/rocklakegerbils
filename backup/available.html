About Adoptions

All gerbils live together in pairs and sometimes trios and quartets. Gerbils will never be homed in singles without prior arragements or discussion about an introduction to a lonely gerbil.

Our babies will be homed no earlier then 6 weeks, unless one is to be introduced to a lonely adult, in which case slightly younger gerbils work better for this situation.

Prices will vary depending on rarity of coat colour, show lines and pair, trio or quartets.

Please do not put adopted gerbils in anything other then a glass aquarium. Cages are not a good home for gerbils and we strongly advice against it. If you need help finding aquariums or glass habitats, check out local ads like Kijiji for used tanks or contact us.

We appreciate that you let us know ahead of time if you are adopting any gerbils from Rock Lake for breeding purposes and are a part of the AGS as a registered breeder or we have made prior arrangements. Thank you!

If at any point in your gerbil's life you feel as though you can no longer keep them, we welcome back any and all gerbils adopted from us. We please ask any returns come with the tanks they are homed in. If you have any concerns after adopting our gerbils or anything you believe could be a problem, please feel free to contact us and we will help as much as we can. Be sure to allow time for your new friends to get comfortable in their environment (and you!)
